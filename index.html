Sample Travel to Cuba in 2023

U.S. travel to Cuba is expected to rebound in 2023, according to a new survey released by the Center for Responsible Travel (CREST). The independent survey of U.S. tour operators and other service providers to Cuba shows that people-to-people travel, study abroad programs and cruise bookings are expected to rise this year after seeing declines in stayover travel in 2022. The findings come in the lead up to the 500th anniversary of Havana as the destination prepares for the expansion of its cultural programming and infrastructure.

The survey showed that 55 percent of respondents involved in people-to-people travel expect their people-to-people travel bookings to Cuba to increase in 2019 compared to 2018.
